Hey!

I have completed my Master's from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay in 2017 under the guidance of Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharya. The focus of my research work was on Natural Language Processing and applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning algorithms to solve the problem of Sentiment analysis, Word Intensity Ranking, Computational Sarcasm. I have also explored a novel problem of Numerical Sarcasm Detection in Social Media Texts by applying Deep Learning Techniques.

Currently, I am working as a Senior Data Scientist in Myntra(a leading Fashion E-commerce Platform in India under Flipkart Group). I am working on problems related to recommendation and personalisation for enhanced user experience. I am also working on representation learning problems using deep learning techniques.
